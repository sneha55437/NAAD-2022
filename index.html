About Us


Music! Its feet stead fasted in the deepest of the seas and its head held high into the sky! Indeed, it’s something so pure, so serene! The melody of the primordial sound that leaves us enchanted, the sound of the cosmos and of human consciousness, a sound that transcends space and time, a sound that has no beginning or end, a sound that isn’t simply music, a sound that’s magic- NAAD! Gear up folks, as Dhwani- Music Club, BIT Mesra returns with its annual inter-college music competition - NAAD! The wait is finally over and the excitement is going to take you on a roll! Get ready to witness music in its purest form through a series of electrifying events and competitions ranging from Hard-core metal rock to spellbinding tunes of Indian Classical Music! Scintillating nights and exhilarating events await! So prepare yourselves to hit the right notes, knock up your instruments to strike the right chords and get ready to flow in a mesmerizing musical journey with us!
