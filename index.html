About Us

Hey Siri, what time is it?

It’s time for the 5th edition of Dhwani's biggest event of the year- "NAAD". It’s time for the battle of the best, for loads of fun and lots of learning. To all the budding performers, Dhwani- Music Club, BIT Mesra brings to you its Annual Fest, a musical platform to present your passion with flair. It will entice enthusiasts with unexpected and complex melodies, simple yet inevitable, and that’s a promise we wish to keep :)

A series of enthralling contests from March 11th to 13th have been designed to unleash the musician in you and astound your worthy opponents with nothing but pure musicality. Broaden your audience and cause wonders with your notes both dark and dulcet, this spring with NAAD'22. Save the dates!!
